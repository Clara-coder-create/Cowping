About Us

Cowpins is sure to be a place you won't forget. Spend your day among the cows. Between cuddling with cows to learning to build shelter with cow menure to a dairy shopping spree, at Cowpins there is something for everyone.

Come on over today, we would be over the (moo)n to have you!

Things To Do

  • Milking: Together with our instructors you will learn how to milk a cow.
  • Cuddling: Cows are extremely loving and calming animals. Enjoy a share of their positive aura during a petting session.
  • Sound Bath: Be transported to Nirvana with our cow sound meditation! Participants will find a quiet spot on the ground and listen to the meditative moos.
  • Manure Building: Get started with new eco-friendly building solutions!
  • Store: Support our farmers by bringing fresh products to your table!
